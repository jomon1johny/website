My Projects

Here are some of the projects I've worked on:

PLANT DISEASE DETECTION

Plant disease detection is a critical aspect of modern agriculture, aimed at identifying and diagnosing diseases affecting plants to ensure healthy crop growth and high yields. By utilizing advanced technologies such as computer vision and machine learning, farmers can quickly analyze images of plants to detect symptoms of diseases, pests, or nutrient deficiencies.

CLINIC MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

A Clinic Management System (CMS) is a comprehensive software solution designed to streamline and enhance the operational efficiency of healthcare facilities. This system facilitates the management of patient records, appointment scheduling, billing, and inventory management, allowing healthcare providers to focus more on patient care.
